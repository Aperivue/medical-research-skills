# BMC Medical Education

## Identity
- **Abbreviation:** BMC Med Educ
- **Publisher:** BioMed Central (Springer Nature)
- **ISSN:** 1472-6920 (online only)
- **Homepage:** https://bmcmededuc.biomedcentral.com/
- **Author guidelines:** https://bmcmededuc.biomedcentral.com/submission-guidelines

## Scope
Publishes research on education and training of healthcare professionals at all levels, including undergraduate, postgraduate, and continuing professional development. Welcomes quantitative, qualitative, and mixed-methods studies on curriculum design, assessment, simulation, and technology-enhanced learning.

## Scope Keywords
medical education, undergraduate medical education, postgraduate training, curriculum development, simulation, assessment, clinical competence, e-learning, interprofessional education, continuing professional development, faculty development, health professions education, educational technology, objective structured clinical examination, competency-based education

## Article Types Accepted
- Research Article
- Study Protocol
- Systematic Review

## Classification
- **Tier:** Q1
- **Open Access:** Full OA (CC BY 4.0)
- **Field:** Health professions education

## Special Notes
High-volume, accessible OA journal for medical education research at all training levels. Pilot/feasibility studies welcome as Research Articles (not Study Protocols). Requires completed reporting checklist (STROBE/SQUIRE 2.0) at submission. AI policy: follows ICMJE — disclose AI use in Methods.
