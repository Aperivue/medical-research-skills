## Scope
Stroke: Vascular and Interventional Neurology publishes original research, reviews, case series, technical notes, and viewpoints on vascular neurology and neurointerventional practice, with emphasis on acute stroke care, endovascular treatment, neurocritical care for stroke, and systems of care. Coverage includes mechanical thrombectomy outcomes, aneurysm and AVM intervention, carotid and intracranial revascularization, telestroke and mobile stroke units, imaging for acute stroke triage, and quality improvement and workflow studies across the vascular neurology / interventional pipeline.

## Special Notes
SVIN is the official journal of the Society of Vascular and Interventional Neurology and an American Heart Association family journal, launched in November 2021 as an online-only, fully open-access publication. It serves the US vascular and interventional neurology community with a faster, OA-first home for thrombectomy outcomes studies, systems-of-care analyses, and smaller registry or single-center series that may not reach Stroke or JNIS. Choose SVIN over JNIS when the manuscript is a single-center or mid-sized cohort in acute stroke intervention or systems of care that benefits from rapid open-access dissemination, or when gold OA is mandated; choose JNIS when the work is a pivotal device trial or high-impact multicenter interventional outcomes study.
