## Scope
International Journal of Stroke publishes original research, reviews, trial protocols, and policy papers spanning the full stroke continuum, with a global health emphasis consistent with its role as the official journal of the World Stroke Organization. Coverage includes randomized controlled trials, large international cohort and registry studies, stroke in low- and middle-income countries, acute stroke treatment and systems of care, secondary prevention, rehabilitation, and WSO position statements. Trial protocols and statistical analysis plans are a recognized format.

## Special Notes
International Journal of Stroke is the official journal of the World Stroke Organization and positions itself as a global venue for stroke trials, trial protocols, and international cohort studies. It is one of the few stroke journals that routinely publishes full trial protocols and statistical analysis plans as a distinct article type, making it a natural home for pre-specifying large RCTs. Choose IJS over Journal of Stroke when the study is an international randomized trial, a pre-specified trial protocol or SAP, or a global health / WSO-aligned policy paper; choose Journal of Stroke when the study is a narrative or systematic review, or an Asian-population observational study where fully open access with no APC is preferred. AI policy: follows SAGE editorial policy aligned with ICMJE — authors must disclose use of AI or AI-assisted tools (including large language models) in the Methods section, and AI cannot be credited as an author.
