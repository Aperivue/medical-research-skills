## Scope
Journal of Stroke publishes original research, reviews, and guideline statements on ischemic and hemorrhagic stroke and cerebrovascular disease, with emphasis on clinically oriented work and high-quality narrative and systematic reviews. Coverage includes stroke epidemiology in Asian populations, imaging biomarkers, acute stroke treatment, secondary prevention, cerebral small vessel disease, intracerebral and subarachnoid hemorrhage, and translational cerebrovascular science. It serves as a regional flagship with increasing international readership.

## Special Notes
Journal of Stroke is the official journal of the Korean Stroke Society and is among the highest-impact open-access vascular neurology journals, publishing three times per year. It is notably receptive to high-quality narrative reviews, state-of-the-art reviews, and systematic reviews alongside original research, and does not charge author fees. Korean IRB approval documentation and Copyright Transfer Agreement with handwritten signatures are required. Choose Journal of Stroke over International Journal of Stroke when the manuscript is a narrative or systematic review, or an observational study with an Asian-population focus, and when full open access with no APC is desired; choose IJS when the work is a large international randomized trial or WSO-aligned global health statement. AI policy: not explicitly stated in the author guidelines; authors should default to ICMJE guidance and disclose any AI or AI-assisted tool use in the Methods section.
